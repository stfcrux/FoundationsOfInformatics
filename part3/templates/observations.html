This heat map shows how many airports are there in each country with the countries colored with darker colours of red based on how many airports there are. From the map we can clearly see that North America has the most number of airports even though its size is rather small. More importantly, from the heat map we can see the general large disparity between the middle of the map and the other two sides. This is rather interesting since one would expect the prevelence of airports in the middle grounds as a common trading and stopping ground, however this is not the case at all. We can also see that generally the capital or more populated countries have the bulk of the airports in each region.
